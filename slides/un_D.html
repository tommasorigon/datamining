  • In this unit we will cover the following topics:

    • Kernel methods and local regression;
    • Regression splines;
    • Smoothing splines.

  • Let us consider again the relationship: Y_i = f(\bm{x}_i) + \epsilon_i, \qquad where \epsilon_i are iid with \mathbb{E}(\epsilon_i) = 0 and \text{var}(\epsilon_i) = \sigma^2.

  • We do not believe f(\bm{x}) is a polynomial or some other parametric function.

  • Can we fit a nonparametric relationship that does not make strong assumptions on f(\bm{x})?
